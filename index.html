PERFUME MANUFACTURING PROCESS

1. Ingredient Selection: High-quality natural and synthetic ingredients are carefully chosen to create the desired fragrance profile.

2. Extraction: Essential oils are extracted from natural sources using methods like steam distillation or solvent extraction.

3. Blending: Perfumers blend various aromatic compounds to formulate the fragrance, balancing top, middle, and base notes.

4. Aging: The blended fragrance is aged for several weeks to allow the components to harmonize and develop depth.

5. Filtration: The aged perfume is filtered to remove any impurities or particulates, ensuring clarity.

6. Bottling: The final product is carefully bottled, labeled, and packaged for distribution.
